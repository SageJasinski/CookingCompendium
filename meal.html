Refrigerator rolls

Ingredients

  • 1 cup shortening
  • 3/4th cup sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 2 Tbs yeast
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 4 eggs
  • 7 1/2 cups of flower
  • 1 cup cold water

Directions

Cream shortening, sugar, salt, add boiling water and stir.

Add yeast softened in warm water.Add flour and water alternate.

Refrigerate 8hrs. Punch down occasionally.

Form rolls let rise. Bake at 375 degrees for 15 min.

Pumpkin Bread

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pumpkin
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup oil
  • 2/3 cup water
  • 3 cups sugar
  • 3 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp clover
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt

Directions

Mix together the pumpkin, eggs, oil, and water. Add sugar.

In a separate bowl sift together remaining dry ingredients and slowly add to the wet mixture. divide into two greased loaf pans and bake at 325 degrees for 1 to 1 1/2 hours.

Pancit

Ingredients

  • 8 oz pancit kanton noodles (yellow)
  • 8 oz rice stick, special bihon
  • 1/3 cup silver swan soi sauce
  • 1/4 cup knorr cablo consabo de pollo
  • 1 whole roasted chicken
  • 5 green onions
  • 2 whole carrots
  • 4 cloves of garlic diced
  • 1/2 small cabbage head
  • 1/2 lb fresh green bean (snap both ends)
  • 2 1/2 cups water

Directions

Dice the garlic and white ends of green onion. shredded the whole roasted chicken and set it aside. Cut all veggies slanted and set aside. No cabbage core. Burner on high. saute garlic and white ends of green onions in 2-3 table spoons of oil until golden brown. soak the rice stick for 15 min in warm water. NEVER SOAK BIHON NOODLES. Put the rest of cut up veggies in the pan to cook. Always stir. Add 3 tbs of black pepper and a little salt in veggie mixture. Add the shredded chicken. Stir in soy sauce. Mi in 1/4 chicken bouillon powder. When veggies are done (still crispy) set them aside. In the next pan, put in the juice from the veggies to boil. Put the soaking rice stick in a strainer. Add rice stick in boiling juice, Stir until cooked. DO NOT LEAVE. Stir always. Add yellow noodles, stir until cooked. Set noodles aside. Put the veggies and chicken mix back on. Stir the noodles in with the veggies and chicken. Stir in completly. Salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with chopped green onions. Enjoy

Mom's homemade bread

Ingredients

  • 5-6 cups whole wheat flour
  • 9 cups white flour
  • 1 3/4 cup dry milk
  • 2 tbs yeast (3 pkg)
  • 1/2 sugar
  • 6 cup luke warm water
  • 3/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 3/4 tbs salt

Directions

Combine Flour, milk, yeast, and sugar. Then add luke warm water and mix vigorously 5 min. Add white flour by the cup till like soupy mashed potatoes. let stand2 20 min, cover with a towel. stir down add oil and salt then add white flour until right consistency, until not to stick to handel. Kneed at least 5 min dont break the skin. Let rise 15 min until doubled in bulk. Make loafs. Let rise till a whole indentation. If it springs back it needs to rise more. Bake at 375 degrees for 35 min.
